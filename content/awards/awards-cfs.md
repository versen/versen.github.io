## VERSEN Thesis Awards - Call for Submissions

***Deadline***: January 28th, 2026

***Submission***: [link](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSeX1CjQAr5c9Sazmtvf4Drl73WiNXPsahRztTc20S-SUSJk5A/viewform?usp=header)

***Process***: low-weight, just upload thesis and candidate CV, and optionally a nomination text and/or link to a video explaining the thesis.

### Procedure Thesis Awards 2025

The Award can only be awarded to graduate students who have obtained their degree from a Dutch institute of higher academic learning between September 1st, 2024, until August 31st, 2025. We strongly recommend that both the winner of a thesis award and the nominator are present at the award ceremony.

### Important Dates (Both Awards)

* 28-01-2026: Deadline for submission

* 25-02-2026: Notification

* There will be a joint PhD and MSc award ceremony at the SEN Symposium, in 2026

### Further Information

For further information about the awards, contact dr. Georgiana Caltais ([g.g.c.caltais@utwente.nl](mailto:g.g.c.caltais@utwente.nl)).
